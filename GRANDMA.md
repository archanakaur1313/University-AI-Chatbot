# My AutoMechanic Project

Hi Grandma! Here is a quick summary of what I've been building:

* It is a friendly digital helper that you can type questions to whenever you have car troubles, like wondering about your oil or squeaking brakes.
* I designed the screen to be very clear and easy to read, so it can be a helpful guide for you even late at night when the regular repair shop is closed down.
* It always knows the current time, so it can greet you warmly right when you ask it for help!

I hope you love it!
